Free Firework Initiative

Bulk Mart, and by extention, Kydonia, hates monopolies, Bulk Mart only absorbs the really big places when possible, to cover the costs of everything else in Kydonia. Fireworks are probably the most sold object due to elytra, and to reduce price fixing, and stop a monopoly on fireworks, we try to make creeper farms (or buy from them), and paper farms to make fireworks and give them away for free.
